A Story About Us

Pempek Wongkito is a proudly Indonesian culinary business founded in 2026, starting from a humble kitchen in Depok, West Java.

Inspired by the rich heritage of Palembang’s traditional pempek, we began our journey with a simple mission: to bring authentic taste and cultural pride into every bite.

Through challenges and the spirit of entrepreneurship, Pempek Wongkito has grown from a small local venture into a brand that represents more than just “jajanan tradisional.”

We aim to be a symbol of how tradition and innovation can blend seamlessly.

Hygiene and Quality is our utmost priority.

Road to Success

five year roadmap

Pempek Wongkito has become an internationally recognized traditional Indonesian culinary brand.
